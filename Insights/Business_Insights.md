# Global Sales & Customer Behavior Analysis Using SQL

### Business Insights & Strategic Recommendations:-

# Executive Summary:-
This analysis evaluates **200,000 global sales transactions** across national and international markets using SQL. The objective was to identify **revenue drivers, customer behavior patterns, geographic performance, and operational inefficiencies**. The insights highlight strong revenue concentration in metro cities, high dependency on select international markets, and operational challenges related to delivery timelines and returns.

-----

# Revenue Insights:-
- Revenue is **highly concentrated in a small number of cities**, indicating that a limited set of urban markets contribute a majority of total sales.
- **India leads in total transaction volume**, reflecting a strong domestic customer base.
- **International markets such as the USA generate higher average order values**, suggesting stronger purchasing power per transaction.
- **Electronics and Fashion categories dominate global revenue**, making them the primary revenue drivers.

-----

# Customer Behavior Insights:-
- **High-value customers contribute disproportionately to revenue** while exhibiting lower cancellation and return rates.
- Customers using the **Mobile App place more orders** than Website users, indicating higher engagement and convenience.
- Customers with repeated completed orders show **stable purchasing behavior**, making them ideal for retention strategies.

-----

# Geographic Insights:-
- **Metro cities consistently rank among the top three revenue contributors** within each country.
- International sales performance is **heavily dependent on 1â€“2 key cities per country**, increasing market concentration risk.
- Domestic markets in India show **more evenly distributed city-level performance** compared to international regions.

-----

# Operational Insights:-
- **Longer delivery times strongly correlate with higher return rates**, indicating logistics inefficiencies.
- Orders with shorter delivery durations show **higher completion and customer satisfaction levels**.
- Payment methods such as **UPI and digital wallets dominate transaction volume**, reflecting a shift toward cashless payments.

-----

# Business Challenges Identified:-
- Over-reliance on **limited cities and regions** for revenue generation.
- **Delivery delays contributing to increased returns**, impacting profitability.
- Underutilization of **non-metro and emerging international markets**.
- Potential revenue leakage due to **cancellations and returns**.

-----

# Strategic Recommendations:-
- Expand marketing and logistics investments in **high-performing cities** while gradually developing emerging regions.
- Optimize supply chain and delivery processes to **reduce delivery timelines and return rates**.
- Strengthen the **mobile application experience** through personalized offers and smoother checkout flows.
- Launch **loyalty programs and targeted promotions** for high-value customers.
- Diversify international market presence beyond current top-performing cities to reduce dependency risk.

-----

# Expected Business Impact:-
- Increased revenue through **better regional and category focus**.
- Reduced operational losses by **lowering return and cancellation rates**.
- Improved customer retention via **targeted engagement strategies**.
- Stronger long-term growth through **geographic market diversification**.

-----

# Conclusion:-
This project demonstrates how **SQL-driven data analysis** can uncover critical business insights across revenue, customer behavior, geography, and operations. The findings provide a clear roadmap for improving profitability, operational efficiency, and customer engagement in a global sales environment.
